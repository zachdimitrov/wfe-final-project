ATP news

For the fourth time in history Roger Federer and Rafael Nadal captured all four Grand Slam titles in a single year. Federer won his 18th Grand Slam defeating Nadal in the final of the Australian Open. Nadal won his 15th Grand Slam, and record 10th title in Paris, defeating Stan Wawrinka in the final of the French Open.
